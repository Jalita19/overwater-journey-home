Our Mission

Stability Starts With Structure.

Overwater Journey Home provides safe, structured supportive housing for individuals rebuilding their lives after incarceration, crisis, or housing instability.

Safe & Supportive Living

A stable, shared living environment designed to promote safety, accountability, and peace of mind.

Structured Program Model

Clear expectations, daily routines, and accountability that support long-term success and independence.

Growth-Centered Community

A respectful, faith-centered environment focused on dignity, growth, and personal responsibility.

Program Standards

Eligibility & Requirements

Independent Living Ability

Applicants must be able to manage daily living activities independently without requiring medical or nursing care.

Program Compliance

Willingness to follow house rules, daily structure, curfews, and accountability expectations.

Income Stability

Must have a current income source or a documented plan to obtain employment or benefits.

Safety & Background Criteria

No history of violent or sexual offenses to ensure a safe environment for all residents.

Commitment to Growth

Demonstrated readiness for personal responsibility, respectful conduct, and participation in the program.

Substance-Free Living

Residents must commit to a substance-free living environment and comply with program testing and accountability standards.

Length of Stay Commitment

Willingness to commit to the program’s minimum length of stay and actively engage in services during residency.

Application & Interview Approval

Completion of an application, screening process, and interview prior to acceptance into the program.
